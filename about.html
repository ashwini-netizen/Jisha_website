Our Mission

Dedicated to Your Spinal Health and Mobility

Jisha Prime Orthopaedic & Spine Care was founded with a single vision: to provide the highest quality orthopaedic care in a patient-centered environment. Located in the heart of Mysore, we serve as a beacon of hope for those suffering from chronic pain and mobility issues.

Our facility is designed to offer a seamless journey from diagnosis to recovery. We believe that every patient deserves a life free from pain, and our team works tirelessly to achieve that through advanced surgical techniques and compassionate rehabilitation.

  • State-of-the-art diagnostic imaging
  • Minimally invasive surgical suites
  • Dedicated post-operative care units

Lead Specialist

Dr. Prajwal Narayan

MS (Ortho), Fellow in Spine Surgery

Dr. Prajwal Narayan is a highly skilled Orthopaedic and Spine Surgeon with over 15 years of experience in managing complex spinal disorders. He completed his graduation and post-graduation from prestigious institutions and underwent specialized fellowship training in advanced spine surgery.

Known for his precision and patient-first approach, Dr. Narayan has successfully performed thousands of surgeries, ranging from simple fracture fixations to complex spinal reconstructions. He is a pioneer in minimally invasive spine surgery in the Mysore region, helping patients recover faster with minimal discomfort.
